Who am I?

My name is Bahman Soltani.

I was originally born in Esfahan, Iran. Currently, I'm a student in Berlin studying for a Master of Computer Science at IU International University of Applied Science. In Iran, I was working as a full-stack developer and software designer specializing in C# and .NET environment, Security programming, Web development (ASP.NET MVC), and SQL Server for 10 years.

I have extensive experience through 10 years of working as a .NET Full-stack developer. From December 2017 to August 2020, I worked as a .NET full stack web developer at Khatsaz Company to implement a web application by inheriting their desktop version application for their new offices in different cities in Iran, which was already implemented by myself from January 2010 to March 2016 as a C#.NET Full-stack developer there, for warehouse management, accounting, human resources, and other administrative tools.

At Varanegar Pishro Company, from June 2016 to November 2017, I worked as a .NET Full Stack Web and Desktop Developer to complete a web-based application for reviewing the completed tasks in different departments for distribution companies and then in another team, to implement a desktop-based application for monitoring the process of the workflows for the distribution companies’ delivery system and then in a bigger team to implement a desktop-based application for discount and reward management for distribution systems.

Having worked in a team-oriented setting and through many projects and training, I have developed excellent communication and interaction skills which enables me to quickly grasp a good understanding of the requirements and tackle day-to-day challenges successfully.
