Who am I?

My name is Bahman Soltani.

I was originally born in Esfahan, Iran. Currently, I'm a student in Berlin studying for a Master of Computer Science at IU International University of Applied Science. In Iran, I was working as a developer with different CRM and EPR systems for about 10 years. My biggest passion is programming and learning new development technologies.

For my undergraduate studies I studied first at the Azad University of Khomeinishahr for an Associate Degree in Computer Science from January 2007 to June 2010 and then for Bachelor in Software Engineering I studied at Azad University of Najafabad from January 2011 to April 2014. After graduating with my Associate's Degree in Computer Science, I started to work as a Desktop Developer at Khatsaz Company and used my knowledge for developing a desktop version of their administrable systems including accounting, management, and warehouse. After finishing their project, I joined Varanegar company's developers' team to work on both windows and web applications. Then after almost two years, I joined back Khatsaz company's IT Department to upgrade their administrative application from windows to a web version for their offices in different cities.
