Who am I?

My name is Bahman Soltani.

I was originally born in Esfahan, Iran. Currently, I'm a student in Berlin studying for a Master of Computer Science at IU International University of Applied Science. In Iran, I was working as a full-stack developer with different CRM and ERP systems for 10 years.

I have extensive experience in C# and SQL Server that I gained through 10 years of working as a software developer. From December 2017 to August 2020, I worked as a full stack developer and the head of the project at Khatsaz Company to implement a web application by inheriting their desktop version, which was done by me in my previous job at the very company, for their warehouse management, accounting, and administrative application. Before that, from June 2016 to November 2017, at Varanegar Pishro Company where I worked as a full stack web developer completing a web-based application for reviewing the completed jobs in different departments for distribution companies, including accounting, stock, sales, and account management departments, and then as a full-stack desktop developer, I implemented a desktop version based on the web version. After that, in another team, as a full stack developer I worked to implement a desktop-based application for administrating and monitoring the process of the workflows for the distribution delivery system. Then I joined a bigger team to implement a desktop-based administrating application for discount and reward management for the accounting department of the central distribution system. And before that, from January 2010 to March 2016 I worked as a full-stack developer at Khatsaz Company to implement a desktop application for their warehouse management, accounting, human resources, and other administrative tools.

After achieving my Associate Degree, I started to work as a full-stack developer at Khatsaz Company, meanwhile, during my work there, I completed my Bachelor of Science Degree in Software Engineering, and after finishing the project at Khatsaz, I joined Varanegar Pishro where I worked as both web and desktop developer.
